5. NEW BIRTH | BAG-ONG PAGKATAWO

John 3:3-8

3 Jesus replied, "Very truly I tell you, no one can see the kingdom of God unless they are born again.[a]" 4 "How can someone be born when they are old?" Nicodemus asked. "Surely they cannot enter a second time into their mother's womb to be born!" 5 Jesus answered, "Very truly I tell you, no one can enter the kingdom of God unless they are born of water and the Spirit. 6 Flesh gives birth to flesh, but the Spirit[b] gives birth to spirit. 7 You should not be surprised at my saying, 'You[c] must be born again.' 7 You should not be surprised at my saying, 'You[c] must be born again.' 8 The wind blows wherever it pleases. You hear its sound, but you cannot tell where it comes from or where it is going. So it is with everyone born of the Spirit."[d]

3 Jesus replied, "I tell you the truth, unless you are born again,[a] you cannot see the Kingdom of God." 4 "What do you mean?" exclaimed Nicodemus. "How can an old man go back into his mother's womb and be born again?" 5 Jesus replied, "I assure you, no one can enter the Kingdom of God without being born of water and the Spirit. 6 Humans can reproduce only human life, but the Holy Spirit gives birth to spiritual life. 7 So don't be surprised when I say, 'You[d] must be born again.' 8 The wind blows wherever it wants. Just as you can hear the wind but can't tell where it comes from or where it is going, so you can't explain how people are born of the Spirit."

3 Si Jesus mitubag, "Sultihan ko ikaw: walay makakita sa Gingharian sa Dios gawas kon siya matawo pag-usab." 4 "Unsaon man pagkatawo pag-usab sa usa ka tawo nga tigulang na? Makasulod pa ba diay siya sa tiyan sa iyang inahan aron magpakatawo pag-usab?" nangutana si Nicodemo. 5 Si Jesus mitubag, "Sultihan ko ikaw: walay makasulod sa Gingharian sa Dios gawas kon matawo siya pinaagi sa tubig ug sa Espiritu. 6 Ang gianak sa tawo, tawo usab. Ug ang gianak sa Espiritu, espiritu usab. 7 Ayaw ikahibulong nga ako nag-ingon kanimo nga kinahanglan magpakatawo kamo pag-usab. 8 Ang hangin mohuros bisan asa kini buot mopadulong; madungog mo ang paghuros niini, apan wala ka mahibalo diin kini gikan o asa kini padulong. Sama usab niini ang tanan nga matawo pinaagi sa Espiritu."

START

If you can turn back time, what do you want to change in your life?
Kung pwede pa lang mabalik ang panahon, unsay gusto nimong usbon sa imong kinabuhi?

DISCOVER

  1. What is the qualification for seeing the Kingdom of God? (3:3)
    Unsay angay mabatunan aron makita ang Gingharian sa Dios?

  2. What did Nicodemus think Jesus meant when He said that a person must be born again? (3:4)
    Unsay anaa sa huna-huna ni Nicodemo sa dihang miingon si Hesus nga gikinahanglan matawo pag usab?

  3. What is required to enter the kingdom of God? (3:5-6)
    Unsay angay natong mabatunan aron makasulod sa gingharian sa Dios?

  4. Why did Jesus compare the wind to the Spirit? (3:8)
    Nganong gipakasusama ni Hesus ang hangin ug ang Balaang Espiritu?

LEARN

  1. How can you say you are born again?
    Sa unsang paagi nga makaingon ka nga natawo na ka pag usab?

  2. What changed attitude have you noticed in yourself since you started learning God's Word?
    Unsay namatikdan nimo nga kausaban sa diha nga nagsugod na ka ug tuon sa Pulong sa Dios?

APPLY

Share specific areas in your life that still needs to be changed.
Ipaambit ang mga kinaiya nimo nga kinahanglan mausab.

Look for someone whom you can share your new life experience.
Pangita ug usa ka tawo nga kapaambitan nimo sa imong bag ong kinabuhi.
