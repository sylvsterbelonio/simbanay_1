ASSURANCE OF SALVATION | KASIGURUHAN SA KALUWASAN

1 John 5:11-13

11 And this is the testimony: God has given us eternal life, and this life is in his Son. 12 Whoever has the Son has life; whoever does not have the Son of God does not have life. 13 I write these things to you who believe in the name of the Son of God so that you may know that you have eternal life.

11 And this is what God has testified: He has given us eternal life, and this life is in his Son. 12 Whoever has the Son has life; whoever does not have God's Son does not have life. 13 I have written this to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, so that you may know you have eternal life.

11 Ang pagpamatuod mao kini: ang Dios naghatag kanato sa kinabuhing walay kataposan ug anaa kini sa iyang Anak. 12 Ang midawat sa Anak may kinabuhing walay kataposan. Apan ang wala modawat sa Anak sa Dios wala makabaton sa maong kinabuhi. 13 Gisulatan ko kamong tanan nga nagtuo sa ngalan sa Anak sa Dios aron masayod nga kamo may kinabuhing walay kataposan.

START

Share three things that you are good at.
Ipaambit ang tulo ka butang nga nakita nimo nga maayo ka (pananglitan, maayo ka moluto, mosayaw, mokanta, etc.)

DISCOVER

  1. What testimony do we need to believe in? (5:11)
    Unsa nga testimonyo ang angay natong tuohan?

  2. What do we have if we have Christ in us? (5:12)
    Unsay anaa kanato kung anaa kanato si Kristo?

  3. What are you assured of when you believe in the Son of God? (5:13)
    Unsay nadawat nimo nga kasigurohan sa dihang mituo ka kang Kristo?

LEARN

  1. How can I know that I have eternal life?
    Unsaon nako pagkabalo nga anaa na koy kaluwasan o kinabuhing walay katapusan?

  2. What hinders me in believing that I have eternal life?
    Unsay mga babag nga di ko makatuo nga duna na koy kinabuhing walay katapusan o kaluwasan?

APPLY

By pair, recite 1 John 5:12 three times.
Pangita ug kauban ug i-recite ang 1 John 5:12

Share this verse with a family member or a friend this week.
Ipaambit ni nga bersikulo sa usa ka kapamilya o higala karong semanahona.
